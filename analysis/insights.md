# Additional Notes on UPI Growth

## Structural Advantage
UPI functions as a public digital infrastructure rather than a private product, allowing multiple firms to innovate on top of it.

## Distribution Dynamics
Competition among apps improved onboarding, incentives, and retention strategies.

## Behavioral Shift
UPI transitioned users from occasional digital payments to habitual usage, indicating strong product-market fit.

## Key Takeaway
Adoption scaled not only because of technology, but because of ecosystem alignment and reduced friction.
